Privacy Policy for Iron Lab Gym

Effective Date: 05/31/2025
Contact Email: dynovexengineering@gmail.com

1. Introduction

Iron Lab Gym ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application, Iron Lab Gym ("App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • QR Code Storage:
    You may upload and store your gym entrance QR code in the app. This code is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Progress Photos:
    Users may choose to upload gym progress photos by entering a passcode. These images are stored only on your device and are not collected, accessed, or shared by us.
  • Workout Logs:
    The app allows you to manually input workout data (e.g., sets, reps, weights used). This data is also stored locally on your device only.

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data—including QR codes, progress photos, and workout logs—is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal or sensitive data to our servers or third parties.

We strongly encourage users to protect access to their device using secure methods such as a passcode or biometric authentication, and to keep backups via their device's native backup service (e.g., iCloud or Google Backup).

4. Third-Party Services

Iron Lab Gym does not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or external services that collect user data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 17+. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 17+. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at dynovexengineering@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

As all data is stored locally on your device, it remains there until you delete it or uninstall the app. We do not retain or store any user data on external servers.

7. Your Rights

Since no data is collected or stored by us, there are no user rights to exercise regarding access, deletion, or correction of data stored on our servers. You have full control over your data directly through your device.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, and the “Effective Date” at the top will be updated. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
